How Runo Works

You can use Runo with any smartphone to make cheap
international calls.

Instead of calling the expensive international number, you call a local
Runo landline number in your country which connects you with the
mobile or landline number of your contact abroad.

We presently have Runo landline
numbers in these 15 countries.

We will launch new countries as the demand increases.

  • Netherland
