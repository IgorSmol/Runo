No internet connection? No problem. With the Runo App you can still make low cost international calls!

Any Network, Any Device, Any Time

You will never have to worry if a family member or friend has poor internet connection, is offline or doesn't have the same App installed.

Features 1 of 6

Save on your monthly data allowance

Runo uses half the bandwidth of Viber or Skype. In many countries mobile data is expensive and supposedly free calls aren't free! Runo means no more unexpected bills plus your battery life and data allowance lasts twice as long.

Features 2 of 6

Your calls will no longer be ignored!

Tired of waiting for friends and family to find a WiFi hotspot because the cost of mobile data stops them taking your calls? With Runo, international calls to mobiles are free.

Features 3 of 6

Share important events with free HD video calls

Connect in real time, without distorted or interrupted live video streaming. With Runo you'll have crystal clear video quality wherever you are.

Features 4 of 6

High quality uninterrupted free voice calls

No more dropped calls, lost connections or annoying distant sounding, muffled or garbled calls. With Runo, barely audible calls are a thing of the past, even if you're using 2G/Edge.

Features 5 of 6

Instant messaging with awesome chat stickers!

When words are not enough introduce colour into your conversation. And, with Runo it's easy to switch between video calls and IM - just swipe the screen, it's that simple.

Features 6 of 6

In seconds, discover stories that are driving conversations

Stay up to date with what your fellow countrymen the world over are talking about. With Runo Trends you'll always have something interesting to say when talking to friends and family.

Keeps You Connected

Runo is on a mission to connect you with your friends, family and business associates, whenever you want, wherever you are.

We're proud of the fact that whether you're in London or New York, a mountain in Africa or a remote village in India, with Runo, you'll always Stay Connected.
